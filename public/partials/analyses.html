In this section you can see all the analyses carried out within the platform. We have divided the analyses in three main categories to facilitate the navigation, examples of the analyses within each category are provided below:

  • Primary analyses

    • Analyses of hardware generated data, machine stats etc.

    • Production of sequence reads and quality scores.

  • Secondary analyses

    • QA filtering on raw data

    • Alignment/Assembly of reads

    • QA and variant calling on aligned reads

  • Tertiary analyses ("Sense Making")

    • Multi-sample processing

    • QA/QC of variant calls

    • Annotation and filtering of variants

    • Data agregation

    • Association analyses

    • Population structure analyses

    • Genome brow-fluidser driven exploratory analyses
